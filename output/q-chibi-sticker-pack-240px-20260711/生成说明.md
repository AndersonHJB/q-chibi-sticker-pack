# Q版表情包生成说明

- 生成方式：`imagegen` 内置图像生成工具（每个表情单独调用一次）
- 人物参考：用户上传的父子照片
- 风格锚点：本批次 `01-早安呀-source.png`
- 最终规格：13 张独立 PNG，240 × 240 像素，纯白背景，方形画布内采用竖向居中人物构图
- 高分辨率源图：`sources/`

## 共享提示词

```text
Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: standalone Chinese chat sticker, one single image only
Input images: Image 1 is the identity and clothing reference photo. Image 2 is the approved character and visual-style anchor.
Preserve the toddler's recognizable short wispy black hair, round face, warm skin tone, olive-green sleeveless top over pale patterned long sleeves. When present, preserve the father's short black hair, broad friendly face, subtle moustache/stubble, and plain white T-shirt.
Scene/backdrop: perfectly clean pure white background.
Style/medium: premium hand-drawn Q-version chibi sticker illustration, soft watercolor-like shading, clean expressive linework, rounded proportions, rosy cheeks, consistent character design.
Composition/framing: square canvas with a portrait-oriented centered composition; generous white margin; all characters and text fully inside the frame; no cropping.
Typography: large playful hand-lettered simplified Chinese, thick rounded strokes, high contrast, white sticker-outline; render the supplied text exactly once and exactly as written.
Constraints: one sticker only, not a collage or sheet; no borders, room interior, photorealism, extra words, or watermark; pure white background; text must remain readable at 240 × 240 pixels.
```
